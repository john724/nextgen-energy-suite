# NextGen Energy Suite ⚡

An advanced portfolio of production-ready smart grid and energy optimization software written in Python. This suite addresses global energy distribution challenges, B2B consumption efficiency, and fleet electrification using algorithmic optimization.

## 🛠️ Portfolio Projects Included

### 1. AI B2B Energy Optimizer (`b2b_energy_optimizer.py`)
* **Problem:** Commercial buildings waste thousands of euros by running high-load hardware during peak-pricing grid intervals.
* **Solution:** Analyzes industrial load profiles against real-time grid spot prices to automatically trigger shaving/shifting actions.

### 2. MicroGrid P2P Energy Trading Engine (`microgrid_p2p_trader.py`)
* **Problem:** Prosumers (consumers with solar panels) sell excess energy back to the utility grid at sub-optimal feed-in tariffs.
* **Solution:** A localized peer-to-peer clearing mechanism allowing neighbors to trade energy inside a virtual microgrid.

### 3. EV Fleet Smart Charging Optimizer (`ev_fleet_smart_charger.py`)
* **Problem:** Commercial EV fleets (delivery, taxis) face high operational costs if charging occurs during peak times.
* **Solution:** A linear-sorting optimization algorithm that structures vehicle charging sessions based on battery requirements and day-ahead market price forecasts.
